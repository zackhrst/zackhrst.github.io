I'm Zack Hurst, a Web Developer

I am a recent graduate from DigitalCrafts Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp. I began my career in technology a couple years ago as a Lead Development Representative at a data security company, Barracuda Networks, where I learned about different security technologies such as Email Security Gateways, Backups, Firewalls, and much more. After a year in the role, I was promoted to Inside Sales Territory Manager in charge of the North East accounts which sped up my learning exponentially. After another year of chasing quotas and being responsible for products I did not build, I decided to make a huge change and try my hand at development. After all, I would rather create than sell another person's creations.

I am an eager learner who works great with others. I have excellent interpersonal communication skills, and I am not afraid to speak with any levels of management.
